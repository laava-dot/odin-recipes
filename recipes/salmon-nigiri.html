Salmon Nigiri

Description

Salmon Nigiri is the most delicious thing there is. It's extremely hard to get it right by yourself, but it's still worth trying. And if it doesn't work out, you can always take a trip to your local sushi place.

Ingredients

  • Raw salmon
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sushi Rice
  • Wasabi (optional)

Steps

  1. Remove the salmon skin. Slice the salmon into small slices. Remember to save the end pieces, you can use them later.
  2. Mix a table spoon of rice vinegar and a table spoon of water.
  3. Make small ball of rice. Remember to wet your hands.
  4. Place the rice ball on top of salmon, press with fingers.
  5. Flip it upside down.
  6. Your first nigiri is ready!
