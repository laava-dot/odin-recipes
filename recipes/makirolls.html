Maki Rolls

Description

Maki Roll is the second delicious thing there is. If you failed to make salmon nigiri, maybe try a maki rolls instead!

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Sushi vinegar
  • Nori seaweed
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger
  • Wasabi (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare sushi rice. Mix it with rice vinagar.
  2. Prepare sushi rolling mat.
  3. Place a sheet of seaweed on the mat, cover most of your nori with your sushi rice.
  4. Add ingredients in a line on top of the rice.
  5. Roll the sushi
  6. Cut the sushi roll.
  7. Enjoy!
